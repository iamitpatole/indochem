Crown plus
Direction of use:
Crown+ is a unique blend of organic amino acid, folic acid.Auxines and vitamins having ability to develop fruits to its optimum level of size, shape, quality and taste.Crown is use full of elongation as well as girth of grapes(Cylindrica shape). For fruit and flower setting in all variety of fruits. Enhances quality, size, luster as well as taste and keeping quality of fruits, Useful to get conical shape in strawberry.
Dose : 0.5 to 1 ml per 1 ltr of water for dip & spray
Packing : 1 ltr
Compatibility :
Compatible with most of the pesticides, fungicide nutrients except those which are alkaline in nature. Please check compatibility before application.
